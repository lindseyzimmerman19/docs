## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Download the app and sign up with your email or social login. It takes less than a minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your skin profile">
    Tell us your skin type and any known allergies or sensitivities. This unlocks personalized ingredient alerts tailored to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan a product">
    Point your camera at any skincare product label or enter an ingredient list manually. Get an instant breakdown of every ingredient.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your skin profile is optional, but setting it up makes your results significantly more useful. Ingredients that are fine for most people may be flagged specifically for your skin type.
</Note>
