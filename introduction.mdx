Skincare Ingredient Scanner gives you a clear, honest look at what's inside your skincare products. Point your phone camera at an ingredient label, and within seconds the app breaks down every ingredient — what it does, how safe it is, and whether it's a concern for your specific skin type. Whether you're managing a sensitive skin condition, navigating a known allergy, or simply trying to make more informed purchasing decisions, the app translates complex INCI (International Nomenclature of Cosmetic Ingredients) names into plain language you can actually use.

## Key features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Safety ratings" icon="shield-check">
    Every ingredient in a scanned product receives a safety rating based on dermatological research and a curated cosmetic ingredient database. Ratings appear on a green, yellow, and red scale so you can spot potential concerns at a glance. See [Safety Ratings](/ingredients/safety-ratings) for a full explanation of how scores are calculated.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Concern flags" icon="flag">
    The app automatically flags ingredients that are common irritants, known allergens, comedogenic (pore-clogging), or associated with specific skin conditions like rosacea or eczema. Flags are surfaced prominently in your results so nothing slips past you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Personalized alerts" icon="bell">
    Set up a skin profile with your skin type (dry, oily, combination, sensitive, normal) and any known allergens or sensitivities. The app uses your profile to adjust which concerns get highlighted — ingredients that are low-risk for most people may still get flagged if they're relevant to your skin.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Plain-language ingredient explanations" icon="book-open">
    Tap any ingredient in your results to see what it is, what it does in a formula, and any associated concerns. No chemistry degree required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Saved products history" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Every product you scan is saved to your history so you can revisit results, track your routine, and compare products side by side. See [Saved Products](/profile/saved-products) to learn how to manage your library.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Who benefits from this app

Skincare Ingredient Scanner is built for anyone who wants to understand what they're putting on their skin, but it's especially useful for:

- **People with acne prone skin** who need to avoid specific ingredients 
- **Those managing allergies or skin conditions** such as contact dermatitis, rosacea, or eczema
- **Ingredient-conscious shoppers** who want to verify clean beauty claims or avoid particular chemical families
- **Parents** evaluating products for children with sensitive or reactive skin
