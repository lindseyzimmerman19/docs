---
title: "Save, Browse, and Compare Your Scanned Skincare Products"
description: "Save scanned products to your library to revisit analyses, track your skincare routine, compare products, and quickly re-check ingredients anytime."
---

Every product you scan can be saved to your personal library for easy reference later. Your library gives you a single place to review past analyses, track the products in your daily routine, and compare options side by side. Instead of re-scanning a product every time you want to check an ingredient, you can open your saved copy in seconds.

## Save a product after scanning

After you scan a product and the analysis loads, tap **Save to Library** at the bottom of the results screen. Before confirming, you can:

- **Add a custom name** — give the product a label that makes sense for your routine, such as "Daily moisturizer", "Evening serum", or "Travel sunscreen". The original product name is pre-filled, but you can change it to anything.
- **Add optional notes** — jot down anything useful, like where you bought it, how your skin reacted, or which routine step it belongs to.

Tap **Confirm** to save. The product appears immediately in your **My Products** tab.

## Browse and organize your saved products

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Browsing">
    Open the **My Products** tab from the bottom navigation bar. Each saved product shows:

    - Its custom name (or original product name if you didn't rename it)
    - The overall safety score as a color-coded rating
    - Any active personal alert badges if the product contains ingredients from your sensitivity list

    Tap any product to re-open the full analysis, including the complete ingredient breakdown, concern flags, and your personalized score.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Searching">
    Use the **search bar** at the top of the My Products tab to find a saved product by name. The search matches both the custom name you gave the product and the original product name from the scan.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Filtering">
    Tap the **Filter** icon next to the search bar to narrow your library by:

    - **Safety rating** — show only products above or below a score threshold
    - **Concern flags** — show only products flagged for a specific concern, such as fragrance or comedogenic ingredients
    - **Skin type compatibility** — show only products that score well for one of your selected skin types

    Filters can be combined. Tap **Clear Filters** to reset the view.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Delete a saved product

To remove a product from your library:

- **iOS** — Swipe left on the product row, then tap **Delete**.
- **Android** — Long-press the product row, then tap **Delete** in the menu that appears.

Deleting a saved product is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Compare two saved products

To compare two products from your library side by side, open one saved product, tap the **Compare** button at the top of the analysis screen, then select a second product from your library. The comparison view shows both products' safety scores, concern flags, and key ingredient differences in a split layout.

<Note>
  When you update your skin profile — whether you change your skin type or add and remove sensitivities — every product in your saved library is automatically re-evaluated against your new profile. The next time you open a saved product, its badges, alerts, and safety score reflect your current settings. You do not need to re-scan anything.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Use the compare feature to make decisions at the store or pharmacy. Save a product you're considering, then compare it against one you already use and trust. The side-by-side view makes it easy to spot meaningful differences in formulation without reading both ingredient lists manually.
</Tip>
